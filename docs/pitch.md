# Enterprise CX Guardian AI - Pitch Deck & Product Pitch

## Problem Statement
For enterprise employees, field agents, gig workers, and travelers, traveling through unfamiliar or high-risk areas introduces safety concerns that impact confidence, productivity, and organizational compliance (duty of care).

## The Solution
Enterprise CX Guardian AI provides real-time, context-aware safety navigation and emergency response:
- **Intelligent Routing**: Evaluates routes based on public risk data, neighborhood safety indices, and real-time user-reported hazards.
- **Active Guardians**: Family, colleagues, or company security dispatch can live-track trips in real-time.
- **One-Tap Emergency SOS**: Instantly triggers high-priority alerts with precise location details, logging to secure DBs, and broadcast via WebSockets and Twilio SMS.

## Target Audience
- Enterprise businesses with field agents, remote employees, or sales representatives.
- Logistics, shipping, and gig-economy operations.
- Corporate business travelers.
- Personal safety applications for individual consumers.
